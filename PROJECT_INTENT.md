# Project Intent (Draft)

This repository serves as a **technical playground** for experimenting with on-chain protocol mechanics and off-chain integrations. It focuses on creating robust, composable, and auditable smart contract systems, exploring the intersection of tokenomics, user asset management, and event-driven design.  

## Current exploration areas

- **Protocol-Level Experiments:**  
  Designing and testing vaults, staking mechanisms, and share-based systems for ERC20 assets, exploring fair accounting, deposit caps, and withdrawal flows.

- **Smart Contract Development:**  
  Prototyping in Solidity (EVM) and Rust (Solana) with a focus on security, efficiency, and modularity.

- **Tokenomics & Accounting:**  
  Evaluating share issuance, proportional ownership, and asset valuation mechanisms, including floor/ceiling logic, cap limits, and accurate math operations using libraries like OpenZeppelin and Math utilities.

- **Off-Chain Integration:**  
  Experimenting with backend systems for tracking events, calculating previews (e.g., `previewDeposit`, `previewWithdraw`), and building tooling for interaction with on-chain contracts.

- **Infrastructure & Reliability:**  
  Exploring deployment strategies, state tracking, and event-driven off-chain computation to ensure consistency and fault-tolerance in real-world scenarios.

- **Tooling & System Boundaries:**  
  Defining system interfaces, constraints, and operational boundaries to identify limitations and optimization opportunities for composable protocols.

> This repository is **not a product or user-facing platform**; it exists purely to capture experiments, validate designs, and explore reliable, scalable approaches for building sophisticated blockchain protocols.
